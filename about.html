About Us

Welcome to NannyMaids: Where Cleanliness Meets Care

At NannyMaids, we believe in the power of a clean home and the importance of quality childcare. Founded in 2019 by Elaine, our company was born out of a vision to provide exceptional cleaning and babysitting services that truly make a difference in the lives of our clients.

Our Story

Elaine's journey began with a simple idea: to create a service that offers more than just cleaning - one that fosters a nurturing environment for families. With a passion for cleanliness and a heart for caring, she set out to build NannyMaids from the ground up.

Our Mission

At NannyMaids, our mission is clear: to deliver excellence in cleaning and childcare services while prioritizing the well-being and satisfaction of our clients. We understand the challenges of balancing a busy lifestyle with maintaining a clean and nurturing home, which is why we're here to provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your needs.
